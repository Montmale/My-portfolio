About

It is a first-person action platformer set in a world of flying pirate ships and floating islands, presented at the "New Game Designer 2024" event at the University of Milan. Developed with Unity in collaboration with computer engineers, my work involved creating 2D/3D graphic assets, character animation, and integrating pre-existing resources.

The game combines exploration, combat, and dynamic mechanics (dash, zip-line, and grappling hooks). The demo begins on an island where a storm has stranded a ship, and players explore a dungeon filled with traps and secrets. They then progress to new levels with unique challenges.

At the heart of Rum 'n' Gun lies the search for a legendary bottle of rum.
