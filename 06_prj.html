About

This speculative project, shown in the Anthropogenic Narratives exhibition at the Milan Triennale, focuses on the theme of global warming. Each group of students identified with a natural element, and we chose fire. We explored its dual nature, both destructive and essential to our progress.

In 2070, where energy fuels are almost gone and prices are very high, there is a great disparity between rich and poor. We developed Flogisto, a service launched by the Ash company.

It uses the combustion of human bodies as energy source, offering money to those who agree to participate. The amount is proportional to the energy value of the body, measured by a special device.
