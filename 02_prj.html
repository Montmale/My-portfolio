About

The purpose of this monograph on the original artist and designer Simonetta Ferrante (Milan, 1930) was to fill the void – of ignorance – which too often characterizes the history of Italian design.

The work started from an in-depth research on her artistic and literary works, made difficult by the scarcity of sources. The leitmotif of the magazine is the sign.

We have tried to convey her style in every choice. The vibrant brushstrokes of her works are balanced by an airy and elegant layout. The dominant color is blue, a symbol of wisdom, peace, femininity. The binding is handmade; the cover reacts to UV rays with a chromatic alteration, from white to blue.
