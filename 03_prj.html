About

This Game Design project reimagines the story of "The Divine Comedy" by Dante Alighieri with a twist: it is not Dante who embarks on the journey, but Beatrice. She starts in Paradise (Level 1), traverses Purgatory (Level 2), and descends into Hell (Level 3) with the goal of saving him. Dante is held captive by Lucifer, who craves Beatrice’s pure soul.

We divided the work into multiple roles: illustration, rendering, and animation. The Lego-style renders were created using 3ds Max and served as narrative support. The 2D game was developed with Illustrator and Phaser.

Will Beatrice manage to defeat the Final Boss, Cerberus—the monstrous three-headed hound loyal to the Fallen Angel?
