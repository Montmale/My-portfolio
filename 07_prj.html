About

This project brought together students from communication, interior, product, and fashion design to reinterpret the bar from Philippe Besson's novel "And the Other Nights, Will You Come?".

We set the story in a future imagined by a man from the far West in the late 1800s. "The Violet Hour" is a bar set on the carriage of a train that travels across the United States every day. The railroad was, in fact, once the symbol of progress.

It is an exclusive bar, intended for first-class travellers, which opens at sunset time: passengers, tired after work, can finally relax and sip cool drinks in a comfortable atmosphere.
