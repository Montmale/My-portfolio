About

Mermaid is an art exhibition that uses p5.js to make the user think about the invisible presence of technology in the modern world. It focuses on passive acceptance of online Terms and Conditions of Use, highlighting the sharing and loss of control of personal data.

The exhibit includes a large projected spiral with stolen data and a QR code for interaction. An ambient sound enchants users, like the voice of a siren.

After scanning the code with their smartphone, users accept without reading the terms. Their camera and microphone data is stolen by Mermaid, disappearing in a spiral of an identifying color. They will be reproduced in the projector graphics.
